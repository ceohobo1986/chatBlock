Join RISC OS now!

You have been a RISC OS user before? Then you know what are the benefits of RISC OS. Do you want to use it again as your primary operating system? Then help us to update RISC OS to the current technical standards.

You are sick and tired of Windows, macOS, IOS, Android or Linux? Then help us to give you another choice for your computer or in the future for your mobile phone.

We already reached the minimum financial goal of our Kickstarter but we still need much more support to achieve all the goals and improvements needed.

One great achievement of our campaign was that we could motivate Simon Wilson to start working on a 3D GPU driver for the RockChip RK3399 boards. These are featured in our Puma Desktop PC and the 14-Laptop. It will be a big step for RISC OS to finally have access to the powerfull GPU features of current SoCs. This could be the door opener for new awesome games in the RISC OS world.
